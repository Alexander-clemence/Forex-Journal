## Setting Your Account Balance

### Why This Matters

Your account balance is crucial because:
- It's used to calculate percentage gains/losses for each trade
- It helps track your overall account performance
- It provides context for your trading results

### How to Set Your Balance

1. **Navigate to Account Balance**
   - Click on "Account Balance" in the sidebar (wallet icon)
   - Or click "Edit Balance" button on the dashboard

2. **Enter Your Base Balance**
   - Enter your starting account balance (the amount you're trading with)
   - This should be your actual account balance before any trades
   - Example: If you have $10,000 in your trading account, enter `10000`

3. **Understanding Balance Types**
   - **Base Balance**: Your manually set starting balance
   - **Trade P&L**: Total profit/loss from all closed trades
   - **Current Balance**: Base Balance + Trade P&L (calculated automatically)

4. **Updating Your Balance**
   - If you deposit or withdraw funds, update your base balance
   - The system automatically adds your trade P&L to show your current balance
   - Click "Edit Balance" or the edit icon to update

### Example

- **Starting Balance**: $10,000 (you set this)
- **Trade P&L**: +$500 (from closed trades)
- **Current Balance**: $10,500 (displayed automatically)

## Adding Your First Trade

### Accessing the Trade Form

1. Click "Add Trade" in the sidebar (plus icon)
2. Or click the "Add Trade" button on the dashboard
3. Keyboard shortcut: Press `Shift + N`

### Required Fields

These fields are **required** to create a trade:

- **Symbol**: The trading pair (e.g., EURUSD, GBPUSD, XAUUSD)
- **Side**: Buy/Long or Sell/Short
- **Quantity**: Position size (in units, lots, mini lots, or micro lots)
- **Entry Price**: Price at which you entered the trade
- **Entry Date**: Date and time of entry

### Optional Fields

You can enhance your trade records with:

**Trade Details:**
- **Exit Price**: Price at which you closed the trade
- **Exit Date**: Date and time of exit
- **Status**: Open, Closed, or Cancelled
- **Stop Loss**: Your stop loss level
- **Take Profit**: Your take profit target
- **Fees**: Trading fees paid
- **Commission**: Broker commission

**Strategy & Analysis:**
- **Strategy**: Your trading strategy name
- **Setup**: The setup type (e.g., "Breakout", "Pullback")
- **Notes**: General notes about the trade
- **Tags**: Custom tags for organization

**Psychology & Journal:**
- **Mood**: Your emotional state (confident, analytical, cautious, frustrated, etc.)
- **Market Sentiment**: Market condition (bullish, bearish, sideways, volatile, etc.)
- **Lessons Learned**: What you learned from this trade
- **Trade Analysis**: Detailed analysis of the trade
- **Pre-Trade Plan**: Your plan before entering
- **Post-Trade Review**: Review after closing
- **Performance Rating**: Rate the trade (1-5 stars)

### Tips for Adding Trades

- **For Open Trades**: Fill in entry details, leave exit price/date empty
- **For Closed Trades**: Fill in both entry and exit details
- **Use Notes**: Document your reasoning and observations
- **Track Psychology**: Record your mood and sentiment - this helps identify patterns
- **Be Consistent**: Use the same strategy names and tags for better filtering

## Managing Trades

### Viewing All Trades

1. Click "All Trades" in the sidebar
2. You'll see a grid of trade cards showing:
   - Trade symbol and side (Buy/Sell)
   - Profit/Loss amount and percentage
   - Entry and exit prices
   - Duration
   - Strategy, mood, and sentiment badges

### Trade Card Features

Each trade card displays:
- **P&L**: Profit or loss in dollars and percentage
- **Percentage**: Calculated relative to your account balance
- **Status Badge**: Open, Closed, or Cancelled
- **Quick Actions**: View, Edit, Delete buttons

### Filtering Trades

Use the search and filter system to find specific trades:

1. **Search Bar**: Search by symbol, strategy, or notes
2. **Filters**:
   - **Status**: Open, Closed, Cancelled
   - **Side**: Buy/Long, Sell/Short
   - **Strategy**: Filter by strategy name
   - **Symbol**: Filter by trading pair
   - **Mood**: Filter by emotional state
   - **Market Sentiment**: Filter by market condition
   - **Date Range**: Filter by entry/exit dates
   - **P&L Range**: Filter by profit/loss amount

3. **Filter Presets**: Save frequently used filter combinations
   - Click "Save Preset" to save current filters
   - Access saved presets from the "Presets" dropdown

### Sorting Trades

Sort trades by:
- **Newest First** (default)
- **Oldest First**
- **Highest P&L**
- **Lowest P&L**
- **Symbol A-Z**
- **Symbol Z-A**

### Editing Trades

1. Click the **Edit** icon (pencil) on any trade card
2. Update any fields you need
3. Click "Save" to update the trade
4. Changes are saved immediately

### Viewing Trade Details

1. Click the **View** icon (eye) on any trade card
2. See a comprehensive view of all trade information
3. Includes all fields, calculations, and metadata

### Deleting Trades

1. Click the **Delete** icon (trash) on any trade card
2. Confirm deletion in the dialog
3. **Warning**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

### Closing Open Trades

1. Edit an open trade
2. Enter the **Exit Price** and **Exit Date**
3. Change **Status** to "Closed"
4. The system will automatically calculate P&L

## Exporting Your Data

### Accessing Export

1. Click "Export Data" in the sidebar (inbox icon)
2. Choose your export format

### Export Options

1. **CSV Export**
   - Export all trades to a CSV file
   - Compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, and other tools
   - Includes all trade fields

2. **PDF Report**
   - Generate a formatted PDF report
   - Includes summary statistics
   - Professional format for record-keeping

3. **JSON Export**
   - Export raw data in JSON format
   - Useful for data analysis or backup

### Date Range Selection

- Select a date range for your export
- Leave empty to export all trades
- Useful for monthly or quarterly reports

### Tips for Exporting

- **Regular Backups**: Export your data regularly
- **Tax Preparation**: Export for tax reporting
- **Analysis**: Export to analyze in external tools
- **Sharing**: Export to share with mentors or coaches
